This website touches up on the fears and concerns most people have with AI intruding on the artspace. An example used in the article is that people will no longer go to artists for "concept art" of themselves for a portrait or an avatar, instead they will upload a selfie of themselves into AI and just have it spit out multiple different variations of the theme they gave it.
